Prawn Board

Rules:

  • Messages are limited to 500 characters.
  • Messages must be original.
  • Users can post 3 times per minute.
  • Only 500 posts are saved, previous posts are deleted.
  • Only 10 posts can be referenced per post.
  • Any referenced post has a maximum of 14 digits.

Features:

  • SQL injection proof.
  • Originality and sentiment are graded by custom algorithms.
  • Friendly, fun, and diverse community.
  • Dedicated janitor cleans up your shit.
